Welcome!

My Dialogue is a personal development tool which will be used by both the employee and the manger to trigger the best development actions and bring ZEAL employees to the next level.

Getting Started
What is MyDialogue?

First, the employee fills out a self-assessment, which consists of Personal and Professional Competencies as well as of Development Actions. The self-assessment will be forwarded to the manager, who then schedules a Development Talk with the employee to discuss the self-assessment and make adjustments if necessary. The final assessment will be uploaded to the Dashboard. Here, a comparison with the complete ZEAL Dashboard can be seen and further development actions can be taken from here. Also, all received feedbacks will be visible in the Dashboard.
